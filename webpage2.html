3-Day Starter Itinerary

A simple framework you can adapt to any city.

  1. Day 1: Landmarks & Orientation

    Start with a walking tour to learn the layout, then visit a top landmark. End with a local dinner spot.

  2. Day 2: Museums & Neighborhoods

    Pick two museums or cultural sites. Explore a distinct neighborhood for lunch and coffee breaks.

  3. Day 3: Nature & Views

    Morning hike or park visit. Sunset viewpoint or river cruise to unwind before departure.

Tip: Book popular sights in advance. Group nearby places to minimize transit time.

Travel Tips

Quick advice to keep trips smooth and fun.

Pack light

Carry versatile layers and comfortable walking shoes. Keep essentials in a small daypack.

Use offline maps

Download maps and transit info for times when data is expensive or unavailable.

Respect local customs

Learn basic greetings and etiquette. A little effort goes a long way.

Budget smart

Track daily spend and prioritize must-do's. Free walking tours are a great intro.
